## Email Pattern Analysis

### Common Email Patterns Identified:

1. **firstname@domain.com** (most common for startups/accelerators)
   - Examples: jonathon@sparklabscultiv8.com, emma@rocketseeder.com

2. **firstname.lastname@domain.com** (common for universities)
   - Examples: vincent.rouillard@vu.edu.au, marlene.cran@vu.edu.au

3. **initial(s)lastname@domain.edu** (some universities)
   - Examples: lmf226@cornell.edu, nl95@njaes.rutgers.edu

4. **role@domain.com** (generic contacts)
   - Examples: info@sproutx.com.au, programs@energylab.org.au

5. **firstinitiallastname@domain.com** (some universities)
   - Examples: m@foodsci.umass.edu
