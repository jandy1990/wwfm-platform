## üö® Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Diminishing Solution Quality
**Mitigation:**
- Strict 5-8 option distribution requirement
- Skip solutions if can't meet quality bar
- Better 42 quality solutions than 50 mediocre ones

### Risk 2: Category Forcing
**Mitigation:**
- Use traffic analysis + research to validate categories
- "Quit smoking" doesn't need "hobbies_activities"
- Stay authentic to what actually works

### Risk 3: Maintenance Burden
**Mitigation:**
- AI-generated = less maintenance than user-generated
- Data is static (distributions don't change often)
- Can deprecate low-performing solutions later

### Risk 4: Scope Creep
**Mitigation:**
- Hard stop after Wave 7 (Top 10 complete)
- Reassess strategy before expanding further
- User data should drive next priorities, not completion
