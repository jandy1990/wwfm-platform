## üî¨ Methodology Notes

### Data Sources Considered
1. **Google Trends** patterns from training data
2. **Public health statistics** (CDC, WHO data)
3. **Market size data** (industry reports, pharma spend)
4. **Social media discourse** volume and sentiment
5. **Clinical prevalence** (diagnosis rates, prescriptions)

### Ranking Criteria Weighted
- **40%** Search volume patterns
- **25%** Market size (commercial + medical)
- **20%** Population prevalence
- **15%** Problem urgency/severity

### Confidence Levels
- **Tier 1 (1-10):** Very high confidence, established data
- **Tier 2 (11-20):** High confidence, strong indicators
- **Tier 3 (21-35):** Moderate confidence, some subjectivity

### Known Limitations
- Training data cutoff (January 2025)
- US-centric bias in search data
- Seasonal variation not captured
- Emerging trends may be underweighted
